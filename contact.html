Common Questions

Frequently
Asked.

Who is eligible for early access?
Early access is available to established research institutions, enterprise organisations, and select government agencies. Applications are evaluated based on intended use case, organisational capability, and alignment with NEXUS deployment objectives.
What does the access programme include?
Accepted applicants receive a dedicated NEXUS unit, full technical documentation, onboarding support from our research team, and ongoing software updates throughout the programme period. A confidentiality agreement is required.
How long does the review process take?
Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. Initial responses are issued within 72 hours. Full evaluation, including background assessment and use-case review, typically takes 7–14 business days.
Is NEXUS available for consumer purchase?
NEXUS-1 is not currently available for consumer purchase. Deployment is restricted to vetted institutional and enterprise partners during the early access phase. Consumer availability will be announced at a later date.
